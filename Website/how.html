How to play

Knowing how to play the game is fairly easy and most people will be able to achieve the rules and objectives of the game within their first go. However, there are some hidden features associated with the game which shall be discussed in this page.

The goal of the game is to type as many words as possble before losing all your lives.

Users can input with their keyboard, and everytime they successfully type a word, they will earn one point, contributing to the progression of the next wave.

If the word reaches the bottom of the form, you will lose one life. If you lose 5 lives, then the game is over.

Submit your score by typing your name into the text box provided when the form appears, to track not only your own progress, but also your friends/family.

Settings can be modified in the settings form that will enable you to customise the game to your liking.

Each wave will have 1 additional word, and each wave will have bigger words too.

The most important part of the game is to have fun, which can lead to improved efficiency in your typing process.
