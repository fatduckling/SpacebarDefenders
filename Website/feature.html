Features & Benefits

Here is a list of all the features that Spacebar Defenders can bring about to the community. These features all combine together to make a positive and beneficial environment to learn typing faster and more efficiently!
  • Interactive home screen - Tank follows your mouse and shoots where you click!
  • Consistent difficulty for all levels of typists as each wave will start off harder and harder. In doing so, novoice players will be more motivated to try harder in order to beat the more experienced players, meaning faster learning skills.
  • Graphics animation - light up the form. Players will play the game longer as it offers more features.
  • Wait time between waves - Users get 5 second wait time to rest their fingers before they progress to the next wave.
  • Saves settings! When application is closed, the settings, such as font size, will be automatically restored along with statistics!
  • Tonnes of customisation - Players can decorate the bullets, fonts and back colors to their liking, which may lead to a greater target market.
  • Audio settings - Users that may not like audio can disable it with a click of a button.
  • In-built help screen - Instantly find out how to play the game with a click of a button.
