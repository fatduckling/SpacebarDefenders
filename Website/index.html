Welcome to Spacebar Defenders!

Spacebar Defenders is written in the powerful language of Visual Basic .NET and brings about powerful features to make learning typing fun and enjoyable for the whole fammily!

In summary, the objective of the game is to protect the space from words that fall from the ground. By typing all the correct letters of the word in exact order, the tank is able to shoot the word, thus preventing a loss and earning you a point.

Stay competitive with family and friends as this game offers tonnes of features that are in utter most and distilled quality. Thank you for playing Spacebar Defenders!

Customisation

Spacebar Defenders allows you to customise many different settings in order to suit your needs! For example, you have the choice to disable sound in order to play in a quiet environemnt and concentrate better to achieve the highest possible score. In addition, font size of the words falling down can be changed by a single drag of a button, so for users with lower eye-sight than others, this problem will no longer persist!
Furthermore, you can change the text color, background color, as well as the bullet color, creating a fun and playful atmosphere, that is both enjoyable and fun, further amplifying the joy of typing!
